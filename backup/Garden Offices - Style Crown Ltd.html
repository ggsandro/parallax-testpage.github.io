    Garden Offices

     

    Welcome to Stylecrown

     

    Garden offices and Garden rooms are made to your own particular needs. We can custom make any of our top designed garden offices to your own requirements, from a simple extra window to a completely new design, just for you.

     

    We make these using quality materials, upvc doors and windows in a wide range of colours, double glazed toughened glass, quality insulation (kingspan or equivalent), firestone Rubber roofing membrane, and quality redwood cladding

     

    Check out our facbook gallery here

     

    Why choose stylecrown?

    All our garden offices, garden rooms, and games rooms are made within our own factory

     

    At stylecrown Ltd, All our garden offices and garden rooms are made within our own factory. We build all our offices ourselves from start to finish. As a result, we can offer greater value for money, keeping production costs lower by hard work, no middle men or sales staff, and ensuring the quality of our work. Every single office is made by a small team of tradesmen, maintaining a high standard of office, at an affordable price. We can manufacture your  office or room, to any shape, size or configuration you want, including multirooms and dual units

    If for example you want your garden office building to be extra insulated, we can fit upto 80mm within our standard walls. If you wanted even more, we can increase the frame size to take upto 120mm! Every detail can be bespoked. 

    With this approach, we feel we feel we can custom make your office to your exact needs.

    Installation

    At stylecrown we construct all of our garden offices and garden rooms by hand, within our factory. They come to site in ready made panels, which we then fasten together. This way we can assure as little disruption to you as possible, and many of our units are finished within a single long working day!

    Security and Maintenance

    With all our upvc doors and windows you get the added security of fully double glazed, toughened glass. They also offer much improved security, with multi-point locking. All our garden offices come with insurance approved 5 lever locks. 

    Internal Finish

    Stylecrown offer a range of finishes. we can do as much or as little as you require in regards to the interior. We can finish your unit to a bare plasterboard finish, to plastered finish, or a fully decorated finish, you decide. This way, our garden offices and garden rooms budget can be spread over time if required. This also allows keen DIYers to do there part if they so wish. We can also offer a timber clad internal finish if required.

    How much will it cost?

    With our 3m x 3m office units starting from as little as £5995 inc VAT and fully installed, we believe we offer great value for money on a quality product. Please see our pricing page
